Open Storage Pod

We are an open source hardware project that is developing cheap, petabyte-scale cloud storage servers built from commodity components.

It all started with a bang when Backblaze — an online backup company — open-sourced their custom storage server design in September 2009:

Petabytes on a budget: How to build cheap cloud storage

“We had to figure out how to store hundreds of petabytes of customer data in a reliable, scalable way — and keep our costs low. After looking at several overpriced commercial solutions, we decided to build our own custom Backblaze Storage Pods: 67 terabyte 4U servers for $7,867.” — Tim Nufire (VP of Engineering, Backblaze)

If you have any questions or want to build your own storage pods and participate in the evolution of the server design, then join the discussion in our Google group.
